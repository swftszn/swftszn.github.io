About

What is Watch Now?

Watch Now is a free & legal Anime streaming service delivering the very best shows, movies and simulcasts direct from Japan. Currently showing 712 shows & movies with thousands of episodes are available to stream, including simulcasting One Punch Man, Dragon Ball Super, My Hero Academia, Attack on Titan, Sword Art Online and many more.

Is Watch Now really free?

Watch Now offers both a Free and Premium option. Checkout our Plans and start watching Anime!
Can you please put my favourite show on the service?

We're always looking for suggestions on what to add to the service. You can visit our Show Suggestion page to highlight a new show or add your vote to others.

Who is developing Watch Now?

Watch Now is a project developed by the team at Madman Entertainment in Melbourne, Australia.

What can I do to help develop Watch Now?

Tell us what you think, warts and all. In the bottom-right corner of each screen there is an blue "Feedback" button, if you see something you like, or something you don't like, or think of something that's missing from Watch Now, hit the button and share your thoughts. Got something on your mind right now? Talk to us.

What countries is Watch Now available in?

Watch Now is only available in Australia and New Zealand. Crazy, huh?

What devices does Watch Now support?

Watch Now is currently available on nearly any device. Visit our Devices page to learn more about how to use Watch Now on your favourite device.

When will Watch Now be available on my device?

If we're not on the device you love, let us know. The team is always working on suporting new and popular devices.
